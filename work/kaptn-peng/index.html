Käptn Peng
LUNA
Type

Music video

Date
2025

For Luna’s music video Käptn Peng we collaborated with Silkrock to develop and implement a pioneering AI workflow centered around photorealistic sheep integrated into real-world environments.

The film tells a coming-of-age and coming-out story built around the metaphor of the “black sheep.” To translate this narrative visually, we generated highly realistic sheep and seamlessly integrated them into the live-action locations, ensuring they felt physically present within the cinematic space.

At the time of production, the workflow represented a significant technical advancement, allowing for a controlled and believable fusion of generative elements and practical footage.

Our scope covered AI development, integration, and post-production through to final delivery.
